What is a digital dog-whistle?

A racist "dog-whistle" is a term used to describe coded hateful language. This language is used to spread hateful ideas, such as homophobia, white supremecy, nazism, racism, etc.. With the popularization and anonimity of the internet, the usage of racist dogwhistles has increased, both as a way of getting around terms of service for certain social media websites, and a way of expressing hateful ideas in a seemingly innocent way. There are multiple ways of referring to this problem, but I prefer the term "digital dog-whistles".
